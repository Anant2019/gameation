About CalmType

Type calm. Type better. Type with purpose.

Our Mission

Most typing tests feel like exams. Ticking clocks, red error markers, competitive leaderboards — they create anxiety, not improvement. We built CalmType to prove that you can get faster at typing while actually enjoying the process.

CalmType is a free, browser-based typing game that combines mindful practice with ambient soundscapes, smooth visuals, and professional vocabulary. No downloads, no signups, no stress.

What Makes CalmType Different

Zen-First Design

Ambient lofi music, particle backgrounds, and soft color themes create a calm space to practice. Your brain relaxes, your fingers speed up.

Professional Vocabulary

Practice with real words you use at work — technology terms, finance language, and policy vocabulary. Every session makes you better at typing what matters.

Three Typing Modes

Zen mode for single words, Flow for sentences, Rush for paragraphs. Progress at your own pace from beginner to speed typist.

Fighter Mode

When you want a challenge, switch to 1v1 Typing Fighter. Type fast to attack your opponent with satisfying combat animations and sound effects.

Works Everywhere

Desktop, tablet, or phone — CalmType adapts to your screen. No app to install. Just open your browser and start typing.

100% Free

No premium tier, no paywall, no account needed. CalmType is free for everyone, forever.

Built With Care

CalmType is an indie project built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The ambient music is procedurally generated using the Web Audio API — no copyrighted tracks, no external dependencies. The 3D background uses Three.js for smooth particle animations that respond to your typing.

We believe the web should have more tools that make you feel good while making you better. CalmType is our contribution.
