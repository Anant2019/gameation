What is CalmType?

CalmType is a free calm typing game built for mindful typing practice. Whether you want to improve your WPM, reduce typing anxiety, or simply enjoy a relaxing typing experience, CalmType gives you a beautiful, pressure-free environment to type in.

Typing Modes

Zen Mode — single word calm practice. Flow Mode — sentence-level typing fluency. Rush Mode — paragraph speed and endurance. All modes include tech, finance, and political vocabulary.

Why practice calm typing?

Calm typing reduces errors, builds muscle memory, and improves focus. CalmType uses ambient sounds and 3D visuals to keep you relaxed and in flow state while you practice.

Our Mission

Most typing tests feel like exams — ticking clocks, red error markers, competitive pressure. We built CalmType to prove you can get faster while actually enjoying the process.

CalmType is a free, browser-based typing game combining mindful practice with ambient soundscapes, smooth visuals, and professional vocabulary. No downloads, no signups, no stress.

What Makes CalmType Different

Zen-First Design

Ambient lofi music, particle backgrounds, and soft color themes create a calm space. Your brain relaxes, your fingers speed up.

Professional Words

Practice with real words you use at work — tech, finance, and policy vocabulary. Every session is productive.

Three Typing Modes

Zen for single words, Flow for sentences, Rush for paragraphs. Progress at your own pace.

Fighter Mode

Type fast to attack your opponent with satisfying combat animations, sword slashes, and impact sounds.

Works Everywhere

Desktop, tablet, or phone — CalmType adapts to your screen. Just open and type.

100% Free

No premium tier, no paywall, no account needed. Free for everyone, forever.

Built With Care

CalmType is an indie project built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The ambient music is procedurally generated — no copyrighted tracks. The 3D background uses Three.js for smooth particles that respond to your typing.

Introduction

CalmType operates the website calmtype.life. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

Information We Collect

CalmType is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No account required — Use CalmType without providing any personal information.
